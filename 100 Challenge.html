ChaseFloydInc 100 Challenge

In my view, the 100 Challenge is the most important and impactful thing Chase Floyd and ChaseFloydInc has ever embarked on. A daring and bold proclamation of improvement, the 100 Challenge is a life-long self-improvement program based around LEARNING, EXPERIENCING, and DOING 100 challenging new things that one has not done before and that most people never will do. Designed to get people to start asking themselves "why not" instead of "why", the 100 Challenge and what it stands for is everything to me. Featured within CFI's Challenge genre, it is the main focus of ChaseFloydInc and thus the main focus of most of my free time. I encourage you to click the above links to learn about each challenge I have done, for I truly believe with every fiber of my being that my program can change the world for the better. I have already seen time and time again how undertaking a single CHALLENGE can drastically change a single PERSON in significantly positive ways, so start today and you too can be on your way UP!
