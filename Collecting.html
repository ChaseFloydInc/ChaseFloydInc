ChaseFloydInc Collecting

Stop Motion

Growing up I was a HUGE fan of action shows and cartoons, and I especially loved collecting the action figures and toys related to my favorites. I also used to eat out a lot with my family, but my main reason for wanting to do this was not for the food but for the TOYS! Over the years I have thus amassed quite the collection of action figures and fast food toys, and as is my nature to hate seeing things and ideas go unused I use items from my collection in many of my Stop Motion films. My work with Stop Motion began with my 7th Challenge, but it turned into something much more and has thus become a sub-genre at CFI!
