ChaseFloydInc Music

Music was one of the original two genres at CFI, and it has thus always had a prominent role in CFI's lineup. To be exact, ChaseFloydInc wouldn't really even exist if it hadn't been for my desire to learn how to play the piano. I got my start by making piano cover videos, and without them CFI never would have gotten off the ground. ChaseFloydInc currently features many music videos, running the gamut from traditional piano covers, original songs, and even some guitar and harmonica!
